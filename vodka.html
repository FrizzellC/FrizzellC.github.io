Vodka Cocktail Recipes

Bloody Mary

Ingredients

Spicy Bloody Mary Mix
  • 4 oz. Sacramento tomato juice
  • 2 dashes Worcestershire sauce
  • pinch of celery salt
  • Pinch of freshly ground black pepper
  • 3 dashes Tabasco sauce
  • 1 squeeze lemon
  • 1 tsp fresh horseradish
Coctail
  • 2 oz. vodka, preferably Finlandia
  • Olives, for garnish
  • Lemon wedge, for garnish

Preperation

1. Make Spicy Bloody Mary Mix: Combine tomato juice, Worcestershire, celery salt, black pepper, Tabasco, lemon, and horseradish. 2. Vigorously shake vodka and bloody mary mix. Garnish with olives and lemon wedge.

Moscow Mule

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 oz. vodka
  • 1/2 oz. fresh lime juice
  • 1/4 oz. simple syrup
  • ginger beer
  • Lemon wedge, for garnish

Preperation

Lightly shake vodka, lime juice, and simple syrup. Add ginger beer and shake. Serve in a copper mug and garnish with lime wedge.

Rooftop Lemonade

Ingredients

  • 2 oz. vodka, preferably Belvedere
  • 3 oz. fresh squeezed lemonade
  • cucumber slices
  • lemon wedges for garnish

Preperation

Shake vodka and lemonade. Toss with cucumber slices. Pour into a Collins glass and garnish with lemon wedges.

Vodka Gimlet

Ingredients

  • 2 oz. vodka
  • 1 oz. fresh lime juice
  • 3/4 oz simple syrup
  • lime wedge for serving

Preperation

Shake vodka, lime juice, and simple syrup. Pour over a coupe and garnish with lime wedge.
